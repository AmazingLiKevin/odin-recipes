Rolled Sugar Cookies

Description

Whenever you make these cookies for someone, be sure to bring along several copies of the
recipe! You will be asked for it, I promise! NOTE: I make icing with confectioners'
sugar and milk. I make it fairly thin, as I 'paint' the icing on the cookies with a pastry brush.
Thin enough to spread easily but not so thin that it just makes your cookies wet and runs off.
